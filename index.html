Our Vision

No pen.
No paper.
Pure creativity.

Art has always been limited by what you can hold — a pen, a brush, a stylus. AirBrush was built to challenge that. We believe creativity shouldn't wait for the right tool to be in your hand.

Using just a webcam and the natural movement of your fingers, AirBrush transforms mid-air gestures into real drawings — and then takes it further, using artificial intelligence to turn those rough sketches into fully realized, photorealistic imagery.

But AirBrush isn't just about drawing. It reimagines how humans interact with technology — replacing passwords and PINs with gestures, signatures, and faces. It's a vision of computing that's more human, more expressive, and more intuitive. We built AirBrush because we believe the most powerful interface is one you already know how to use: your own body.

✦ Core Canvas

🖐️

Air Drawing

Draw in real-time using hand skeleton tracking via webcam. Zero touch required — just move your finger through the air.

🖱️

Mouse Mode

Switch seamlessly to classic mouse drawing on the same canvas whenever you prefer traditional input.

🤖

AI Image Generation

Convert your rough sketch and a text description into a stunning photorealistic AI-generated image instantly.

🎙️

Voice-to-Text

Describe your artwork using your voice. The Web Speech API auto-fills the description box hands-free.

↩️

Gesture Controls

Shake your fist to undo. Raise two fingers to activate the eraser. Edit your canvas without touching anything.

🎨

Full Toolkit

Colour picker, brush size control, eraser, undo, and clear — all neatly organised in one side panel.

✦ Authentication System

✍️

Signature Auth

Register and log in by drawing your unique air signature — tracked by your webcam.

🔢

Finger PIN

Set a 4-digit PIN using raised fingers. Each digit is auto-detected after a 3-second stable hold.

🔷

Pattern Auth

Draw a custom gesture pattern as your password — traced through the air.

👤

Face Recognition 2FA

Every login is paired with facial recognition for dual-layer biometric security.
